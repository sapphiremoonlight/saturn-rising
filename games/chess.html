How to Play

  • Click one of your pieces, then click a highlighted legal square.
  • Pawns move forward 1 square (2 on first move) and capture diagonally.
  • Rooks move straight, bishops diagonally, knights in L-shapes.
  • The queen combines rook + bishop movement.
  • The king moves one square in any direction.
  • You cannot capture your own pieces.
  • The game ends when a king is captured.
  • Undo/Redo lets you rewind moves (player moves only).
  • Winning earns points shown in the lobby.
  • Difficulty affects how aggressively the computer plays.
